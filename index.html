Laroche Glaucoma Risk Calculator

Estimates glaucoma risk per eye using three factors: Age, Intraocular Pressure (IOP), and Central Corneal Thickness (CCT). Scoring bins and risk thresholds reflect the companion PDF. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Risk guidance per PDF: Sum of points **0–5 → Low risk (re-evaluate in 1 year)**; **6–18 → High risk (complete ophthalmic evaluation ASAP)**. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Scoring bins

Age → points
≤40: 0 | 41–50: +1 | 51–60: +2 | 61–70: +3 | 71–80: +4 | 81–90: +5 | ≥91: +6
IOP (per eye) → points
≤12: 0 | 13–15: +1 | 16–18: +2 | 19–21: +3 | 22–25: +4 | 26–29: +5 | ≥30: +6
CCT (per eye, μm) → points
>600: −3 | 576–600: −2 | 551–575: −1 | 526–550: 0 | 500–525: +1 | 475–499: +2 | 450–474: +3 | 425–449: +4 | 400–424: +5 | <400: +6

Note: CCT thicker than 600 μm reduces risk (negative points). Bins align to the table in your PDF. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
